New installations

NEAT is a specialist in providing an end-to-end solution for any type of weighing requirements. From a light weight grocery scales to weighbridges and weigh pads, the team engineers and technicians at NEAT are specialists in complete installation to give your business the cutting edge.

Importing and Distribution of all kinds of brand new scales

We directly import all kinds of scales from leading brand names around the world. We are also the sole distributor for a number of household brands in Sri Lanka. At NEAT, you can also order a specific device of your choice and we will do the importation and other related services for the particular device for you.

All Scales Repair and Maintenance

We do complete repairs and overhauls of any kind of weighing scales. In addition we are trusted for years in maintaining any type of scale systems on contract basis where the client does not suffer the overhead of proper maintenance of their equipment.

All spare parts

We will supply and import all spare parts not only for the scales we have delivered but also for any scale system you may already possess. All spare parts come with genuine warranty and NEAT can also take care of installing the newly arrived spares for your machinery.

Scale fabrications and customization

At NEAT, we are experts in planning, customizing an fabricating scale systems upon client’s requirement. We focus on delivering sustainable and cost-effective solutions where our clients can enjoy the maximum benefits from these systems in the business.
