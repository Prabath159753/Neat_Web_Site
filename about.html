About Us

The One-Stop-Place For All Your Weighing Solution Needs

We, At Neat Weighing Scale Systems, Are A Specialist In Delivering Ideal End-To-End Solutions For All Types Of Weighing Needs. Ranging From Simple Grocery Weighing Scales, More Sophisticated Analytical, Density And Moisture Scales To Weighbridges And Weigh Pads, Neat Weighing Scale Systems Has Been One Of The Pioneers In The Industry For The Past 18 Years.

Neat Weighing Scale Systems (Pvt) Ltd Was Established In 2004 By Mr. Mahesh Amila , A Vastly Experienced Technician And An Expert Widely Known In The Industry. Neat Has Grown Ever Since With His Vision Of Delivering Most Technically Advanced And Top Quality Weighing Solutions And Services In Sri Lanka.
